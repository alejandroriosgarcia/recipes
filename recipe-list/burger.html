Burgers!

Description

This is a recipe for a classic American burger! Nothing special about it, when you think burger this is what you should picture in your mind. Not too many ingredients, and can be dressed up or down however you like!

Ingredients

  • 2 Burger Buns
  • 4 oz 80/20 Ground Beef Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Sliced American Cheese
  • Raw Onions

Steps

  1. Slice lettuce, tomatoes, and onions to desired thickness. Set aside for later.
  2. Place your 4 oz patty on the grill.
  3. Once the patty is halfway cooked, flip over. Let it cook until it's almost fully cooked through.
  4. Place your slice of american cheese on top while the patty finishes cooking so the cheese can melt on top.
  5. Toast both of your buns until they're crispy.
  6. Assemble! Bottom bun first, patty and cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, then top bun in that order.
